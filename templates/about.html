About Us

Welcome to our DL-NAD website. We are a dedicated team of four students enrolled at Misr International University, specializing in Computer Science with a profound focus on cybersecurity. Under the expert guidance of Dr. Sahar Abdelrahman and Eng. Mohamed Khaled, esteemed leaders in the field, we embarked on a transformative journey to develop a sophisticated Intrusion Detection System (IDS) as part of our graduation project. We seek to encourage digital landscapes against the ever-evolving threats of cyber attacks. At the core of our solution lies the intricate integration of advanced Deep Learning models, meticulously engineered to discern and classify network packets with unparalleled precision. Our dedication to research is exemplified by our utilization of industry-renowned datasets, including UNSW-NB15 and KDD-CUP. Moreover, we are proud to announce that our project is generously sponsored by Orange Business, a global leader in digital solutions. Through relentless exploration and unwavering determination, we aim to not only elevate the discourse surrounding cybersecurity but also to tangibly contribute to its advancement. We invite you to join us on this transformative quest towards a safer and more secure digital ecosystem.
